Animal Vaccination Table

S.No Animals Vaccination Details
1 Cattle
  • Clostridial Diseases: Calves: Initial dose at 2-4 months, booster 4-6 weeks later. Annual boosters.
  • Respiratory Diseases: IBR, BVD, PI3, BRSV: Initial dose at 2-4 months, booster 4-6 weeks later. Annual boosters.
  • Leptospirosis: Annual vaccination, especially in areas with a history of leptospirosis.
  • Brucellosis: Often done at a specific age, depending on local regulations.
  • Parasite Control: Regular deworming based on herd health and environmental factors.
2 Dog
  • Bordetella (Kennel Cough): Frequency depends on risk, often annually.
  • Leptospirosis: Initial series, then annual boosters.
  • Lyme Disease: Dependent on geographic risk, may be annual
3 Goats
  • Tetanus (CD/T): Kids: Initial dose at 8 weeks, booster 4 weeks later. Adults: Annual booster.
  • Overeating Disease (CD): Kids: Initial dose at 8 weeks, booster 4 weeks later. Adults: Annual booster.
  • Caseous Lymphadenitis (CL): Depending on the risk in the area, vaccination may be considered.
  • Caprine Arthritis Encephalitis (CAE): Consider testing and vaccination if needed.
  • Enterotoxemia (Type C and D): Kids: Initial dose at 8 weeks, booster 4 weeks later. Adults: Annual booster.
4 Horses
  • Tetanus: Foals: Initial dose at 6-8 weeks, booster 4-6 weeks later. Adults: Boost every 10 years.
  • Eastern and Western Equine Encephalomyelitis (EEE/WEE): Foals: Initial dose at 4-6 months, booster 4-6 weeks later. Adults: Annual booster.
  • Rabies: Initial dose at 4-6 months, booster 1 year later, then every 1-3 years depending on local regulations.
  • West Nile Virus (WNV): Foals: Initial dose at 4-6 months, booster 4-6 weeks later. Adults: Annual booster.
5 Cats
  • Rabies: Initial dose at 12-16 weeks, booster 1 year later, then as per local regulations.
  • FVRCP: Start at 6-8 weeks, boost every 3-4 weeks until 16 weeks, then every 3 years.
  • FeLV: Consider based on risk. Start at 8-12 weeks, booster after 3-4 weeks, then as needed. Regular vet check-ups for a tailored plan
