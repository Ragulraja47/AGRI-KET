Raising Hens - Seasonal Guide

Summer Season - Raising Hens

Week Age Maintenance Vaccination Disease Risks
1 0-7 days - Keep brooder at 32-35°C to ensure warmth.
- Provide a clean, dry environment.
- Change bedding regularly. 		Marek's Disease - Marek's Disease
- Salmonellosis
- Fowl Pox
2 7-14 days - Reduce brooder temperature to 29-32°C.
- Monitor chicks for stress.
- Provide clean water and high-quality starter feed. 		Infectious Bursal Disease (IBD) - Infectious Bursal Disease
- Newcastle Disease
3 14-21 days - Keep brooder temperature at 26-29°C.
- Ensure proper ventilation.
- Avoid overcrowding. 		Newcastle Disease - Coccidiosis
- Infectious Coryza
- Infectious Bronchitis
4 21-28 days - Reduce brooder temperature to 24-26°C.
- Introduce perches and light stimulation.
- Provide grit for digestion. 		Infectious Bronchitis - Infectious Coryza
- Fowl Pox
- Mycoplasmosis
5 28-35 days - Maintain ventilation.
- Provide additional space as chicks grow.
- Monitor for heat stress. 		Fowl Pox - Fowl Cholera
- Avian Encephalomyelitis
6 35-42 days - Transition to grower feed.
- Ensure proper ventilation.
- Avoid overcrowding. 		Newcastle Disease - Newcastle Disease
- Fowl Pox
- Mycoplasmosis
7 42-49 days - Transition chicks to adult coop.
- Provide more space and nesting areas.
- Ensure proper waste disposal. 		Avian Encephalomyelitis - Avian Encephalomyelitis
- Pullorum
- Infectious Laryngotracheitis
8 49-56 days - Continue providing grower feed.
- Ensure adequate space.
- Monitor for signs of heat stress. 		Infectious Coryza - Infectious Coryza
- Avian Influenza
9 56-63 days - Provide additional enrichment activities.
- Encourage natural behaviors.
- Monitor for respiratory illness. 		Pullorum - Pullorum
- Coccidiosis
- Mycoplasmosis
10 63-70 days - Increase space for growing hens.
- Continue grower feed.
- Monitor for signs of stress. 		Coccidiosis - Coccidiosis
- Salmonellosis
- Infectious Coryza
11 70-77 days - Transition to layer feed if appropriate.
- Maintain proper sanitation.
- Ensure proper ventilation without drafts. 		Avian Influenza - Avian Influenza
- Infectious Bronchitis
12 77-84 days - Ensure proper coop sanitation.
- Monitor for signs of heat stress.
- Provide proper ventilation without drafts. 		Mycoplasmosis - Mycoplasmosis
- Infectious Coryza
- Avian Influenza
